- Concept of an ideal man is shattered, and strives for an equilibrium.

- Moderation is key.

- In this poem, the most important qualities of a man, according to the author are depicted.

- The author gives advice to his son, talking about the qualities wich every man should have if he wishes to be looked up to.

- The author understands that a man should seek balance, acceptance, patience, confidence, mixed with ambition and truthfulness in order to 
become someone that anyone can look up to.
